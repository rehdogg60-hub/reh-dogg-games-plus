# 🔍 ARCADE WORD SEARCH

### ⚡ REH DOGG GAMES PLUS ⚡

A fast-paced, combo-fueled word puzzle game. Find hidden words before time runs out!

🎮 **Play the game:** [https://rehdogg60-hub.github.io/reh-dogg-games-plus/arcade-word-search/](https://rehdogg60-hub.github.io/reh-dogg-games-plus/arcade-word-search/)

---

## 🎯 HOW TO PLAY

Drag your finger (or mouse) across the letter grid to highlight a word. If the word is on your list, you score points and build your combo! Wrong guesses cost a life.

- **Find all words** on the list to complete the level
- **Build your combo** by finding words quickly (resets after 3.5 seconds)
- **Finish early** to earn a time bonus
- **3 lives** – make too many wrong guesses and it's game over

| Feature | Details |

| Time Bonus | Extra points for finishing early |

## 📂 CATEGORIES

Choose from 5 different word categories:

- 🚗 **Cars** – Mustang, Ferrari, Lamborghini, etc.
- 🏍️ **Motorcycles** – Harley, Ducati, Yamaha, etc.
- 🐘 **Animals** – Tiger, Zebra, Kangaroo, etc.
- 🍺 **Beers** – Stout, Lager, IPA, etc.
- 🔫 **Guns** – Glock, Shotgun, Revolver, etc.

Each category has 10+ words. Each round randomly selects 7–9 words to find.

---

## 🕹️ CONTROLS

- **Touch / Mouse Drag:** Highlight letters to form a word
- **Release / Lift finger:** Submit the word
- **Category Buttons:** Switch word themes
- **NEW GAME Button:** Restart current category
- **SOUND Button:** Toggle arcade sound effects

---

## 🎨 TIPS FOR HIGH SCORES

1. **Build your combo early** – the multiplier applies to every word you find
2. **Don't guess randomly** – wrong guesses cost lives AND reset your combo
3. **Scan for short words first** – quicker finds keep the combo alive
4. **Finish with time remaining** – each second left adds 50 points to your score
